Events

Past events
  • First clinical meet was conducted on 21st March 2014 at Hotel Himalaya Horizon Dhulikhel. This program was appreciated by all and was a good platform for enhancing the knowledge and amusement. It was indeed a memorable and a mile-stone event of our society.
  • Second clinical meet was held on 19th July 2014 in Army hospital. There was an encouraging participation from the periodontists, prosthodontist, general dental practitioners. This overwhelming response encouraged and motivated for us to move ahead.
  • On 18th October 2014, our society conducted a grand CDE program at Hotel Radisson on “Periodontics and “Prosthodontics”. This program was conducted in joint collaboration with CCDE. Prof Dr Balaji Manohar and Prof Dr Omkar K Shetty were the Guest speakers in this session.
  • Promoting knowledge through different academic activities was the primary aim of this society. With this novel aim we conducted 1st PG convention at NAMS on 15th November 2014. There was active participation from the residents of all the post graduate institutes.
  • On 21st March 2015, our first Annual Conference in collaboration with Indian Society of Periodontology was conducted an Dhulikhel Resort, Dhulikhel.
  • 3rd Clinical Meeting was conducted at Kathmandu Medical College, Sinamangal, Kathmandu on 15 August 2015.
  • On 10th October 2015, CDE programme was conducted in joint collabration with Nepalese Prosthodontic society at Yellow Pagoda Hotel, Jamal, Kathmandu.
